Our Story

BORN TO
SERVE THE SKIES

From a single agreement with Vayudoot in 1986 to representing 7 airlines across the globe.

Formerly known as United Air Travels and Aerolincs Aviation Services, Skylincs secured its first business contract on 13th March 1986 — ground handling the inaugural Hyderabad–Goa–Hyderabad flight. Today, we are one of India's largest independent Ground Handling Companies, with presence across UAE, UK, Portugal and Bulgaria, carrying an unwavering commitment to quality, empathy, and service excellence.
